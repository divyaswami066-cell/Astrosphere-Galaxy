Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. About 29.2% of Earth's surface is land, while the remaining 70.8% is covered with water.

💡 Did You Know?

Mind-blowing facts about the mysteries of outer space

🪐

A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus. It takes Venus longer to rotate once on its axis than to complete one orbit around the Sun!

- Venus Fact
⚡

Jupiter is a massive cosmic vacuum cleaner. It is so big that it could fit over 1,300 Earths inside it, and its gravity attracts passing asteroids.

- Jupiter Fact
🌀

Neptune has the strongest winds in the solar system, reaching speeds of up to 2,100 kilometers per hour—faster than the speed of sound!

- Neptune Fact
☄️

There are millions of asteroids orbiting the Sun. If you clumped all of them in the main belt together, they would still be smaller than our Moon.

- Asteroid Fact
